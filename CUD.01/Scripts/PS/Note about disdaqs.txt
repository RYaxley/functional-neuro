DISDAQ INFO FOR DIFFERENT GROUPS

CUD.01
Adolescents have 4 volumes removed from their image files. Higher-level
analyes will use PS/4disdaqs data. They also have 4 * 2(TR) = 8 s removed from
behavioral data.

ExecInfo
Adults E1 have 0 volumes removed from their image files. Higher-level analyes
will use PS/0disdaqs data. They do have 4 * 1.5(TR) = 6 s removed from
behavioral data.

Adults E2 have 0 volumes removed from their image files. Higher-level analyes
will use PS/0disdaqs data. They do not have any time removed from behavioral
data.
